Research

I'm interested in computer vision, machine learning, optimization, and image processing. Much of my research is about inferring the physical world (shape, depth, motion, paint, light, colors, etc) from images. Representative papers are highlighted.

Deep Artifact Rejection in Microscopic Urinalysis

Worked towards my thesis to develop and implement robust deep learning models to support the detection of unknown/unseen objects during prediction. Models were tested on a microscopic urine sample image dataset, where multpiple artifacts present in the images were identified and rejected at test time, while also correctly classifying clinically significant objects. Techniques from the open set recognition and out-of-distribution detection literature were explored, in addition to our own clustering experiments in feature space.
